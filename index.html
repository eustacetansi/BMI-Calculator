KNOW YOUR B.M.I TODAY

Body Mass Index is a simple calculation using a persons's height and weight. The formular is BMI = KG/(M*M) where kg is a persons's weight in kilograms and (m*m) is the height in meters squared. A BMI of 25.0 or more is overweight, while the healthy range is 18.5 to 24.9. BMI applies to most adults 18-65 years.
